Home Automation

By Students of B.E. Computer Engineering

Home automation is the use and control of home appliances remotely or automatically. It refers to the automatic and electronic control of household features, activity, and appliances. In simple terms, it means you can easily control the utilities and features of your home via the Internet to make life more convenient and secure, and even spend less on household bills. It uses a combination of hardware and software technologies.
