About SCET IoT Club

The SCET IoT Club was formed in 2016 under Computer Engineering Department with guidance of expert faculties, IoT Club guide the students to build the new things and brings up with new ideas and solution everyday, It provides you a platform to build your technical skills, when it was started in 2016 the great immense response gotten from students by creating projects & exploring new hardware technologies and ideas getting to solve real life base problem.

Vission

IOT Club at Computer Engineering Department provides the platform to the students with the following visions:
○ To Enhance the Leadership skills
○ To explorer interdisciplinary knowledge
○ To give the exposure of new technology
○ To showcase the technical skills and share the knowledge among peers
○ To build stronger community which can learn in team resulting in enhanced coding skills with hardware

Activities Done in IoT Club

○ Various workshops are organised for students to make awareness to IOT , Practical experience with hardware , to share advance technologies related to other domain like robotics, drone, Computer vision.
○ Students Developed some projects on various domains viz.
○ Gestures based car
○ Gestures based home automation
○ Classroom projector Automation
○ AC Automation
○ Object or human tracking drone
○ Classroom light/fan automation
○ Drone communication
